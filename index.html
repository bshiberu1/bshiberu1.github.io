Our Goal

Coronavirus Disease 2019 (COVID-19) caused by the Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome Coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2) novel coronavirus has created a path of death, healthcare chaos, and economic instability as it has ravaged the globe as a pandemic. We consider and integrate data from reported COVID-19 cases, social distancing metric, and various socioeconomic status variables, and use data-driven approach to assist policy making.

Based on the work of a similiar project for the city of San Jose in CA (HTTP://BAY.STANFORD.EDU/COVID19)
